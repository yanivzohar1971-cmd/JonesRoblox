## Module Responsibility

admin: live ops, release waves, admin control.

bank: deposits, withdrawals, transfers, financial state.

business: player-owned business flow.

career: jobs, shifts, promotions.

credit: credit score, loans, repayment.

economy: world economy ticks, inflation, market state.

education: courses, skill progress, credentials.

events: life events, choices, outcomes.

health: energy, condition, recovery.

housing: rent, apartments, moving.

housingMarket: pricing, availability, demand.

inventory: items, food, tools, owned assets.

messaging: NPC/player communication.

npcs: citizens, roles, schedules, behaviors.

production: production-ready gates and stability.

reputation: social and city reputation scoring.

risk: negative events, financial risk, safety.

social: relationships and social actions.

stocks: market investments.

system: overview, diagnostics, world state summary.
