## Exact Studio steps — Home at your house

### 1. Find the house
1. **Edit** mode (not Play)
2. **Explorer** → **Workspace** → browse models (House, Building, etc.)
3. Click the building in the viewport or Explorer
4. Press **F** to focus the camera on it

### 2. Add the zone Part
1. **Home** tab → **Part** → **Block**
2. **Explorer** → rename to **Zone_Home** (F2)

### 3. Move it over the house
1. Select **Zone_Home**
2. **Move** tool (**Ctrl+2**)
3. Drag it over the **walkable home area** (yard, door, rooms) — **not spawn**

### 4. Scale it around the house
1. **Scale** tool (**Ctrl+3**)
2. Size about **30×15×30** or larger so you can stand inside/on the porch
3. Cover where the player will **Rest**

### 5. Set properties
| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Anchored** | ✅ on |
| **CanCollide** | ☐ off |
| **Transparency** | 0.5 |

### 6. Save
**File** → **Save to Roblox**

---

## Play test — Rest

1. rojo serve → **Sync**
2. **Play** (F5)
3. At spawn: **Current Zone: Industrial** → **Work Shift** (optional, −20 Energy)
4. **Walk to your house** (not spawn)
5. Enter the semi-visible **Zone_Home** box
6. HUD: **Current Zone: Home**
7. Click **Rest** → Rest complete! +30 Energy.
